AI is concerned with the design and implementation of computer systems capable of solving problems that usually require the ability of human beings. Such problems correspond to a high complexity or to natural tasks (e.g. vision or natural language understanding). They usually cannot be solved by classical algorithmic methods. For solving them, AI programs mainly manipulate symbolic information and not only numerical data as usual in computer science. AI makes an extensive use of various kinds of knowledge about a domain of application. Therefore the problems of knowledge acquisition, representation and use are central in AI research and development. The implementation of knowledge-based systems represents one of the most active branches. An important characteristic of AI is its pluridisciplinarity. AI has strong relationships with computer science, but the development of AI projects necessitates also notions coming from logic, linguistics, cognitive sciences, etc. Besides, AI is used in a large variety of application fields: medicine, industry, bank and finance, etc.
